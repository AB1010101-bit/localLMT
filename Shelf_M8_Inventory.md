## Special Safety Protocols

### **Cyanide Compounds (Ferrocyanide/Ferricyanide):**
- **NEVER mix with acids** - Can generate deadly HCN gas
- Work only in fume hood
- Cyanide antidote kit should be nearby
- Emergency shower access required

### **Potassium Hydroxide Handling:**
- **SEVERELY CAUSTIC** - Can cause severe chemical burns
- Use face shield and chemical-resistant gloves
- Emergency eyewash station access
- Work in well-ventilated area

### **Oxidizer Precautions (Iodate, Nitrate):**
- Keep away from organic materials
- No smoking or open flames
- Store separately from reducing agents

---

## Emergency Procedures

### **Cyanide Exposure:**
1. Remove from exposure immediately
2. Fresh air and oxygen if available
3. Contact poison control: **1-800-222-1222**
4. Seek immediate medical attention

### **Caustic Burns (KOH):**
1. Flush with copious water for 20+ minutes
2. Remove contaminated clothing
3. Seek immediate medical attention
4. Do NOT neutralize with acid

### **General Spills:**
1. Evacuate area if large spill
2. Use appropriate neutralizing agents
3. Clean up in fume hood if possible
4. Dispose as hazardous waste

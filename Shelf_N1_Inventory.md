## Critical Safety Protocols

### **Hydrazine Hydrate - EXTREME CAUTION:**
- **CARCINOGENIC** - cancer risk with exposure
- Work only in fume hood with full PPE
- No smoking or open flames nearby
- Emergency shower access required

### **Hexane-1,6-diamine Handling:**
- **SEVERE CORROSIVE** - causes burns
- Use face shield and chemical-resistant gloves
- Avoid inhalation - work in ventilated area
- Eye wash station must be accessible

### **Organic Acid Precautions:**
- Corrosive to skin and metals
- Work with good ventilation
- Neutralization materials available

## Emergency Procedures

### **Hydrazine Exposure:**
1. Remove from exposure immediately
2. Flush with copious water for 15+ minutes
3. Seek immediate medical attention
4. Contact poison control: **1-800-222-1222**

### **Amine Burns (Hexanediamine):**
1. Flush with water for 20+ minutes
2. Remove contaminated clothing
3. Do NOT neutralize with acid
4. Seek immediate medical attention

### **Organic Acid Spills:**
- Neutralize with sodium bicarbonate
- Ventilate area well
- Clean up in fume hood if possible

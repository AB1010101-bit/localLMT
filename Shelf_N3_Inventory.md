## Critical Safety Protocols

### **Nitro Compounds - EXPLOSION RISK:**
- **NEVER allow to become completely dry**
- **Shock-sensitive when dehydrated**
- Store with slight moisture content
- Handle with extreme care - no impact/friction

### **Carcinogenic Compounds:**
- **Naphthalene** - work in fume hood only
- **Nitrobenzene** - full PPE required
- Avoid skin contact and inhalation
- Cancer monitoring protocols required

### **Corrosive Amine (N-Butylamine):**
- **SEVERE BURNS** to skin/eyes
- Generates toxic vapors
- Work in fume hood with full PPE
- Emergency shower access mandatory

## Explosive Material Precautions

### **Dry Nitrophenol Compounds:**
- **EXTREMELY DANGEROUS** when moisture content <5%
- Can explode from slight shock or friction
- Must be kept slightly moist at all times
- Professional disposal recommended

### **Handling Protocol:**
1. Never move containers roughly
2. Check moisture content before handling
3. Add distilled water if appears dry
4. Transport in padded containers

## Emergency Procedures

### **Nitro Compound Emergency:**

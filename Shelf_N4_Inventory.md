## Special Safety Protocols

### Perchloric Acid
- Use ONLY in perchloric-rated fume hood with washdown system
- Avoid organics/combustibles; prevent residue accumulation in ducting

### Phenol / Phenol Solution
- Causes deep chemical burns; rapid dermal absorption
- Wear face shield + chemical-resistant gloves; immediate decontamination for contact

### Hydrazines (Methylhydrazine, Methylhydrazinium chloride)
- Probable carcinogens; handle in hood with full PPE
- Segregate from oxidizers; use sealed secondary containment

### Flammable Aldehydes and Gases
- Ground equipment; eliminate ignition sources
- Store aldehydes cold and tightly sealed; secure propane cylinders

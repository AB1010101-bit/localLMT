## Safety Information

### Hazard Classifications
- **Sodium chlorate**: Strong oxidizer, fire/explosion risk
- **Sodium bismuthate**: Oxidizer, potentially toxic
- **Soda lime**: Caustic, causes burns
- **Most sodium salts**: Generally low toxicity but hygroscopic

### Storage Requirements
- Keep sodium chlorate away from combustible materials
- Store soda lime in dry conditions
- Ensure good ventilation for all compounds
- Check older chemicals for degradation signs

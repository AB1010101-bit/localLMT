## Special Safety Protocols

### **Halogenated Compounds (Iodo-compounds):**
- **LIGHT-SENSITIVE** - store in dark containers
- **ALKYLATING AGENTS** - potential carcinogens
- Work only in fume hood
- Use chemical-resistant gloves

### **Methylamine Solutions:**
- **GENERATES TOXIC GAS** - work in fume hood only
- **CORROSIVE** - causes severe burns
- **FLAMMABLE** - no ignition sources
- Emergency ventilation access required

### **Flammable Solvents (Multiple alcohols/esters):**
- **FIRE HAZARD** - large quantities present
- No smoking or open flames
- Static electricity precautions
- Fire suppression equipment nearby

## Emergency Procedures

### **Fire Emergency:**
1. Evacuate area immediately
2. Call fire department - inform of halogenated compounds
3. Use appropriate fire suppression (not water on solvents)
4. Ventilate area after fire suppression

### **Chemical Exposure:**
- **Iodoform/halogenated:** Remove from exposure, seek medical attention
- **Methylamine:** Flush with water, emergency medical care
- **Metaldehyde:** Poison control immediately: **1-800-222-1222**

### **Spill Procedures:**
- **Small spills:** Absorb with inert material in fume hood
- **Large spills:** Evacuate, professional cleanup required
- **Never use water** on iodine bromide spills
